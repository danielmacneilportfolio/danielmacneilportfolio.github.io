Alyx Studios Project Submission

The following project was created while I was a contender for a graphic design position at 1017 Alyx 9SM in spring 2019. Under guidelines from one of their employees I was to create textured shirts with their 1017 9SM logo, hand written versions of the word 'Alyx' for graphic knitwear, and jewellery concepts. I included a page of graphic designs for the brand, which are shown on hoodies. This is the first fashion project I have created. Unfortunately I didnt get the position, but I'm proud that I was was given a shot to work at a company like Alyx. There is a ton of space for growth, and I'm looking forward to it.
